Wangs Garden is a small family owned and operated restaurant that opened in 2002 in downtown durham. Married owners Wen Duan Wang and Wai Kam Chan decided to start their life here after immigrating from Fujian province in China and learning the tricks of the trade in Brooklyn, New York. They have been proudly serving New York style Chinese food for over the past 20 years and have stayed loyal to their recipes.
